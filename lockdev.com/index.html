01. Lockwitz Development

builds custom web applications for your organization.

Our business is dedicated to following industry best practices when it comes to creating online solutions. Lockwitz Development will gather your requirements, design and review a solution with you, implement the solution and verify that your application is working as desired. Our software development process guarantees that you will be satisfied.

Our services:

We build custom applications using the latest in web technology. HTML5, CSS3, PHP5, MYSQL 5.5, Zend, HAML, SASS, WordPress 3 and more are the building blocks of your web application.

The web continues to develop at a rapid pace and you need a company that is fluid and cutting edge. Using web service APIs from today's data leaders like the Facebook, Google, Yahoo, and more we build software that stays relevant to the changing world.

Questions lead to answers. We encourage you to contact us just for more information. We will provide open and honest answers to your questions. We want your business, but only if you need our services. Call today to see how Lockwitz Development is the right choice for you web application.
